R.F. MacDonald Co. — Industrial Equipment Services

The Problem

Paper forms, manual entry, zero visibility.

R.F. MacDonald Co. is one of the largest boiler and burner service companies on the U.S. West Coast, with 12 offices across California and Nevada serving commercial, industrial, and institutional customers.

25 field salespeople were generating 100–125 quotes per month each. Every quote started on paper or a tablet, then got manually entered into NetSuite by inside sales.

That's 50–100 hours of data entry per salesperson per month. Errors, bottlenecks, and zero visibility for managers into what the field team was actually doing.

The Solution

A full production sales management application.

Not a prototype — a system used daily by a real company. Built from discovery call to production deployment by one person.

  • • 7 digitized form types with auto-calculation engines (pump quotes, skid quotes, AM/EMP estimates, BPO assessments, ANSI pump RFQs)
  • • 31-table PostgreSQL database with Row-Level Security on every table
  • • Multi-role access: admin, manager, field sales, inside sales
  • • Approval workflow: draft → submit → review → approve/reject
  • • Customer and equipment inventory, linked to jobs
  • • Executive dashboard: KPIs, trends, status distribution across all offices
  • • Mobile-first PWA, installable on phones for field use

Results

What changed.

Digitized paper-based sales workflow across 12 offices
Real-time manager visibility into field sales activity for the first time
Automated quote calculations that previously required manual spreadsheet work
Approval pipeline that eliminated bottleneck between field and office
Designed to save the company 1,000+ hours per month in manual data entry
Delivered beyond contract scope: labor rates admin, printable reports, CSV export, audit logging
